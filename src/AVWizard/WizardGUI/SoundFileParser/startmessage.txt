That wizard will set up Orbiter to work on your computer. 
For doing that you should keep in mind that right arrow 
will increase your settings and left arrow will decrease them.
If you have more than one item on your display you may 
navigate using up-down arrow. When you are agree with your
settings you should press enter. If you are agree with 
current settings you must press Enter key to save them 
or Escape to restore the defaults.
Press Enter to continue or Escape to quit
